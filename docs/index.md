## Important principle

An alert is a starting point for investigation, not automatically proof of cheating.

The strongest decisions normally combine:

- Detection context
- Repeated behaviour
- Timeline evidence
- Server-side logs
- Staff observations
- Player history
- The possibility of lag, desynchronisation or resource conflict
